Does data conform to a standard - normally distributed data
1-Sample t test
mean = standard

Are the averages for two groups of normally distributed data the same?
2-Sample t test
mean group 1 = mean group 2
mean group 1 not = mean group 2
Are the averages for three or more groups of normally distributed data the same?
One Way ANOVA test
mean of all groups are the same
at least one group's mean is different than the others
Does the median of a group of non-normal data comply to a standard
1-Sample Wilcoxon test
median not different than the standard

Comparing median of two groups of data, used for non-normal data
Mann-Whitney test
median group 1 = median group 2
median group 1 not = median group 2
Comparing median of three or more groups of data, used for non-normal data
Mood's Median test, for when distribution of each group's data has similar shapes
group 1 = median group 2 = median group 3
at least one group's median is different from the others
Comparing median of three or more groups of data, used for non-normal data
Kruskal-Wallis Median test, for when distribution of each group's data has different shapes
median group 1 = median group 2 = median group 3
at least one group's median is different from the others
